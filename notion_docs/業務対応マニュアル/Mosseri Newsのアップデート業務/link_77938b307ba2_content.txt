概要
Toolkit for Instagram
A very simple toolkit for Instagram™.

Desktopify is a extension designed to enhance your Instagram™ experience. Currently, it allows you to effortlessly open and dowload images📷 in high-definition (HD) quality. Whether you're a content creator, a designer, or simply an Instagram™ enthusiast, this feature helps you access and save high-quality images with ease.

Previously named InstaDesktop.This toolkit is constantly evolving and will soon include more exciting features to help you get even more out of Instagram™. Stay tuned for updates as we add tools to improve your productivity, creativity, and overall experience on the platform.

This app requires only the minimal permissions required to work to improve your security.
