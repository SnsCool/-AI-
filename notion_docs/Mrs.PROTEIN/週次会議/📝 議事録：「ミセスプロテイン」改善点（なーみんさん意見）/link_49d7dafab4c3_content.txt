AND THE FRIET, a French fry specialty store that can be lined up in Hiroo, Tokyo, has developed a “dried frit” premium snack that makes use of the yellow pulp and umami of Belgian potatoes over several years! Please enjoy seasonal limited products and rich drinks containing half of the whole raw lemons released only by GINZA SIX.
